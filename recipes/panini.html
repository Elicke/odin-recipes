Panini

Description

A grilled sandwich that goes well with chips, soup, or a salad!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 slices Italian bread
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 3 slices deli ham
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 slice tomato
  • 1 thin slice onion
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Drizzle olive oil on 1 side of each bread slice.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon mayonnaise on the other side of each slice.
  3. Arrange layers of ham, cheddar cheese, tomato, and onion on top.
  4. Top with other bread slice (mayonnaise side down).
  5. Heat a grill pan over medium-high heat and spray with cooking spray.
  6. Transfer sandwich to grill pan and weigh it down with a heavy pan.
  7. Cook 3 to 5 minutes per side.
  8. Serve!
