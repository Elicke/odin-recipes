French Toast

Description

With the recipe below, you can learn how to easily prepare delicious and fluffy French toast (which isn't actually French!).

There's no better way to start your morning than with this tasty dish -- let's get right into it!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • salt
  • 6 slices bread
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Lightly butter a skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Dunk bread in the egg mixture.
  4. Transfer bread to the skillet and cook 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve!
